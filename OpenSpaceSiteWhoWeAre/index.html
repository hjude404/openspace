Coming Together...

In the beginning, three unique personalities came together to effectuate a purposeful vision to enhance the educational experience in classroom. Kensley, Gary, and Judah joined forces to tackle a common problem found in the classroom. Kensley, was the visionary who came up with the idea to design an app where students can feel free to ask questions and be involved in the classroom without being judged or ridiculed for their questions. Judah, whose leadership and coding skills helped keep us on task and brought our ideas to life. Gary, the charismatic and vivacious entity in the group, helped his team members be motivated and energized!
