Who is OpenSpace For?

OpenSpace is chiefly aimed at university students. Big lecture halls often make the prospect of asking questions seem daunting, with so many people there; OpenSpace provides, well, an open space for students to ask their questions without insecurities of being jusdged or ridiculed by their peers--but it doesn't end there.

What if you're late for class? Or you miss a class? Use OpenSpace! You can get right up to speed in a flash by just asking questions. Examine other student's questions, search for certain keywords, and ask your own questions; OpenSpace's top priority is easing some aspect of the already stressed student.
